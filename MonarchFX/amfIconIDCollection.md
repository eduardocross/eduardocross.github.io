The IconID collection contains all of the valid strings for the <code>[IconID](amfDdsButtonClassIconIdProperty.html)</code> property. It is divided into four categories: Apps, Text Editor, Directional, Video Player, Social, Medical. The full listing is displayed below, and further details can be found at [ASNA.Monarch.WebDspF](http://fortawesome.github.io/Font-Awesome/icons/"> http://fortawesome.github.io/Font-Awesome/icons/.</a>
